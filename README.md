## 🚀 Latest Release: White Paper on Contemporary China (July 2026)

**EN:** *The Theory of Rent Extraction and Contemporary China: Understanding the Land Nodal Point and Preparing for Strategic Transformation*  
**中文:** *收租理论与当代中国：理解土地节点与准备战略转型*

> **Author / 作者:** Bruno DE DOMINICIS (Based on the Theory of Rent Extraction by Thierry REBOUR)  
> **Date:** September 2025, revised July 2026

**EN:** This white paper applies the Theory of Rent Extraction (TdR) to analyze China's current structural economic transition. It demonstrates that China is approaching the **nodal point** of its land rent extraction cycle. Absolute rent is absorbing systemic value, leading to falling returns on capital and a spreading real estate crisis. The report proposes operational policy recommendations—aimed at destroying the old rent's capacity to capture wealth through "creative destruction"—to initiate a new cycle toward new dominant scarcities: energy, life resources, data, and AI.

**中文:** 本白皮书运用收租理论（TdR）分析中国当前的结构性经济转型。报告指出，中国正逼近土地收租周期的**节点**。绝对地租正在吸收系统价值，导致资本回报率下降和房地产危机蔓延。报告提出了一系列可操作的政策建议——旨在通过“创造性破坏”打破旧地租攫取财富的能力——从而向能源、生命资源、数据和人工智能等新的主导稀缺性开启新周期。

## 📖 Overview / 概述

**EN:** This repository presents Thierry Rebour's economic theory, examining how dominant scarcity shapes economic dynamics and systems. Economics is understood here not just as a market mechanism, but as gradient fields generated by the morphodynamics of dominant scarcities.

**中文:** 本仓库呈现蒂埃里·雷布尔的经济理论，研究主导稀缺如何塑造经济动态和体系。经济学在此不仅被理解为市场机制，更被视为由主导稀缺的形态动力学所产生的梯度场。

## 🧠 Core Concepts / 核心概念

### Theory Nomenclature / 理论命名

- **EN**: "Rent Extraction Theory" (accurate translation, *not* "redemption")
- **中文**: "收租理论" / "主导稀缺理论"

### Conceptual Framework / 概念框架
- **Economics as Gradient Fields** / **经济作为梯度场**: How dominant scarcity morphodynamics generates economic flows / 主导稀缺的形态动力学作为经济流的起源。

## 🔍 About the Comparative Study / 关于比较研究

The *France-China-Russia Study* applies Rent Extraction Theory to four pivotal historical configurations, revealing how the release or blockage of land access by elites determines whether societies enter cycles of growth or collapse.
*法中俄研究*运用收租理论对比四大历史案例，揭示精英阶层对土地资源的开放或封锁如何决定社会走向繁荣周期抑或系统性崩溃。

- 🇫🇷 **Post-war France (1945–1975)**: Mass popular access to land triggered the *Trente Glorieuses*.
- 🇨🇳 **Ming China (1368–1644)**: Elite refusal to release land fueled peasant revolts and dynastic collapse.
- 🇨🇳 **Post-Deng China (1978–2010)**: Controlled rural land-use rights unleashed productivity.
- 🇷🇺 **Contemporary Russia (1991–2025)**: Authoritarian rent extraction approaching a structural nodal point.
